DANCE OF DEMOCRACY"

India with its population exceeding one biilion, stands as the largest democratic country in the World. As a sovereign, socialist, secular and democratic Republic India operates under a parliamentary system of a government.

India's government is loosely modelled on the British Westminister system.

It consists of a president as head of state, and executive headed by the prime minister, a legislature consisting of a parliament with an upper and lower house (the Rajya Sabha and Lok Sabha) and a judiciary with a Supreme Court as its head.
