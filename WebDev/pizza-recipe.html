HOW TO COOK A PIZZA

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Sauce
  • Toppings of your liking
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Flatten the Pizza Dough
  2. Add the sauce to the dough
  3. Add the toppings and cheese
  4. Bake till cooked

Things to consider

Allergies
Gluten and Lactose
Calories
Very high in calories
